Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for developers and designers who need to ship fast feedback.

Floating webcam bubble

Transparent, always-on-top circle visible across all Spaces and full-screen apps. Drag it anywhere.

Screen & window capture

Live thumbnail picker for any display or app window. Your last selection is remembered.

Camera & mic control

Pick a specific device, use system default, or go silent. Choices persist between sessions.

Quality presets

Choose at export time — High (CRF 16), Medium (CRF 22), or Low (CRF 28). FFmpeg H.264 with +faststart.

Preview & trim

Dual-range scrubber with live timestamps. Fine-tune start and end to the decimal second before exporting.

Fully local & private

Nothing leaves your machine. No telemetry, no servers, no sign-up. Your recordings are yours.

Record in three steps

Sits in your menu bar. Ready when you are.

1

Pick your source

Click the tray icon. Select a screen or app window from live thumbnails. Choose your camera and mic. Hit Start Recording.

2

Record & stop

Your floating webcam bubble appears. Record for as long as you need — chunks stream directly to disk so memory stays flat. Click Stop Recording when done.

3

Trim & export

Scrub the preview, drag the trim handles to cut the clip, pick a quality preset, and hit Save & Compress. MP4 lands in your chosen folder.

Free & open source, forever
